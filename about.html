About Venkatesh Thummisetti

Venkatesh Thummisetti is a multidisciplinary athlete, performer, and artist who moves at the intersection of skill, creativity, and self-mastery. He is a highliner, climber, snowboarder, longboarder, cliff jumper, paraglider, fire dancer, LED performer, juggler, martial artist, and freestyle/ecstatic dancer — constantly exploring the edges of human potential through movement, art, and flow.

Born and raised in India, and later living in the U.S. for nearly a decade, Venkatesh’s journey has been one of transformation — from the logical world of math and corporate life at Amazon to a lifestyle of embodied play and artistic expression. What began with solving puzzles and Rubik’s Cubes evolved into mastering movement arts, fitness, and flow disciplines.

The first spark came from his own physical transformation — dropping weight, gaining strength, and discovering the power of consistent growth. That inner shift led him into flow arts: juggling, staff spinning, poi, and eventually fire and LED performances that turned practice into performance, and discipline into joy.

Today, Venkatesh shares his skills and creativity in three ways: as a coach, helping people discover strength, balance, and creative confidence through fitness, calisthenics, slackline, and flow; as a performer, bringing the magic of fire and LED flow props to events, festivals, and celebrations to spark wonder and create memories; and as a visual artist, through custom Rubik’s Cube mosaics — one-of-a-kind pieces that blend logic and beauty.

Ultimately, his mission is to make transformation and artistry accessible to everyone — whether through movement, creativity, or the mesmerizing patterns of fire and color that remind us to live in flow.
